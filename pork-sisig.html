Pork Sisig

A popular Filipino dish made of sizzling pork, often served as a bar snack or main dish.

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Pork belly (boiled and chopped) 500g
Onion (finely chopped) 1 medium
Chili peppers (sliced) 3-4 pieces
Lemon (or Calamansi) 2-3 pieces
Mayonnaise 1/4 cup
Cooking oil 2 tbsp
Salt and pepper To taste
Soy sauce 2 tbsp
Garlic (minced) 4 cloves

Instructions

1. Boil the pork belly until tender. Once tender, chop into small pieces.
2. In a hot pan, sauté garlic, onion, and chili peppers until fragrant.
3. Add the chopped pork belly and cook until slightly crispy.
4. Add soy sauce, salt, and pepper to taste. Stir well to combine.
5. Remove from heat and mix in the mayonnaise.
6. Transfer the sisig to a sizzling plate and squeeze lemon or calamansi juice over the top.
7. Let it stay on heat for awhile before serving.
8. Make sure there are no seeds on top, and make sure the chili is sat perfectly.
9. Carefully lift your sizzling plate.
10. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Pork Sisig!
