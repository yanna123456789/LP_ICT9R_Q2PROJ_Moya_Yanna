Pancit Canton

A classic Filipino stir-fried noodle dish, perfect for celebrations like Christmas and Birthdays.

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Pancit noodles 500g
Chicken breast 200g
Pork (optional) 100g
Carrots (julienned) 1 medium
Cabbage 1/2 head
Garlic (minced) 4 cloves
Onion (chopped) 1 medium
Soy sauce 3 tbsp
Cooking oil 2 tbsp

Instructions

1. Cook the pancit noodles according to package instructions.
2. In a large pan, sauté garlic and onion until fragrant.
3. Add the pork and chicken and cook until browned.
4. Add the carrots and cabbage, and stir-fry for a few minutes.
5. Add the soy sauce and cook until vegetables are tender and well mixed.
6. Toss in the cooked pancit noodles, mix well, and adjust seasoning as needed.
7. Find a good plating for this dish.
8. Get a number of Calamansi and squeeze gently around the dish.
9. Let it sit for a few minutes.
10. Serve hot and enjoy!
